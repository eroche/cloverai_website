Our story

Our beginnings, and journey to becoming industry leaders

At Clover.AI, we have decades of combined experience in natural language processing as an object of scientific inquiry and in large-scale, high-availability industrial deployments in more than thirty languages. While LLMs have allowed extraordinary breakthroughs in the last few years, they also face significant hurdles. Namely, they are costly to train and run, large, and have no internal logical representations, which means it’s challenging to constrain them significantly. Natural Language contains an immense trove of information that LLMs have discarded entirely to relearn everything from scratch. While this has simplified the problem tremendously and allowed the progress we have witnessed, it has also led to the hurdles we just mentioned. At Clover.AI, we designed the CLOVER programming language to extract meaningful language information and reinject it into smaller, more efficient, and more logical LLMs.
